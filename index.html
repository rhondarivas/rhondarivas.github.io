About Me

I have been away from web development for sometime now. I first started back in 1997 when HTML was the "thing". I started a fan based website for Titanic and met many life-long friends as a result. Life happened and temporary jobs turned into permanent jobs. My focus changed. I have now been able to return to design and photography . Thanks in part to myself for making the leap and partly due to my employer, Chipotle Mexican Grill and their tuition benefits. I have a love of design and creativity along side photography. I am enrolled in Bellevue University Web Design and have worked on a few things within the class. I am still a work in progress with learning JavaScript, CSS and all the many new options that are out there in the Web Devlopment world. I am learning Github and I get better at at on a daily basis but many of the tools we are leaning in our classes are new to me. I enjoy the creativity and potential of creating fresh content. I hope to incorporate my love of writing and photography when I completed with my degree. That is why I have chosen photos of cameras as my photo links, the background is to show off my love of the beach and summer. Writing is a goal of mine as well. The sky could be the limit with these creative outlets I have within me! Until then, I will continue to learn, tweak pages, take photos, and write.
