About Me

I have been away from web development for some time now. I first started back in 1997 when HTML was new and was the "in thing". At first I began designing graphics, and put the graphics to wor on sites. Soon, Anndawson, a fan-based website for Titanic (1997) was born and thus started a life of photography, design and coding. Throughout this journey I met many life-long friends. Anyway, Life happened, and temporary jobs turned into permanent jobs. My focus changed after a few years at my job and I drifted away from design,

I have now been able to return to design and photography. Thanks in part to myself for making the leap and partly due to my employer, Chipotle Mexican Grill and their tuition benefits. I have a love of creativity, photography, travel and writing. I feel like I am returning to my coding roots. I am still a work in progress with learning JavaScript, CSS and all the many new options that are out there in the Web Development world. GitHub and I are getting along better, and I improve a daily basis. All of the design tools we are leaning in our classes are new to me. I enjoy the creativity and potential of creating fresh content.

I hope to incorporate my love of writing, travel and photography when I completed with my web development degree. That is why I have chosen photos of cameras as my photo links; the background is to show off my love of the beach and summer. The sky could be the limit with these creative outlets I have within me! Until then, I will continue to learn, tweak pages, take photos, and write.

I look forward to learning more coding and other development products to best design sites and services!
