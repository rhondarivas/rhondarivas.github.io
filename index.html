About Me

I have been away from web development for sometime now. I first started back in 1997 when HTML was the "thing". I started a fan based website for Titanic and met many life-long friends as a result. Life happened and temporary jobs turned into permanent jobs. My focus changed. I have now been able to return to design and photography . Thanks in part to myself for making the leap and partly due to my employer, Chipotle Mexican Grill and their tuition benefits. I have a love of design and creativity along side photography. I am enrolled in Bellevue University Web Design and have worked on a few things within the class.
